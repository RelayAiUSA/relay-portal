Questions answered

Everything you need to know.

Is a credit card really required for the free trial?
Yes — and we want to be upfront about that. Your card is required to start the Essential+ or RelayPRO trial. If you cancel before the 14 days are up, you will not be charged a single cent. We ask for it upfront so you have a seamless upgrade experience if you love it — which we're confident you will. Starter at $19 has no trial and charges immediately.
What happens when the trial ends?
Your card will be charged automatically at the plan rate ($49/mo for Essential+, $99/mo for RelayPRO). You'll receive an email reminder before the trial ends. If you cancel before then, no charge — ever. You can manage billing, cancel, or downgrade anytime from your portal dashboard.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use RelayAI?
Not at all. If you can send a text message, you can use RelayAI. We built it specifically for contractors and tradespeople — not accountants or software engineers. Setup takes under 10 minutes and every screen is designed to be self-explanatory.
How does AI document generation work?
You upload your blank document template — a service agreement, job completion form, estimate sheet, whatever you use — once. Every time a job is submitted, RelayAI reads the job details and fills in your template automatically, creating a custom, professional document for that specific customer. Available on Essential+ and RelayPRO.
Does RelayAI work with my accounting software?
Yes. RelayAI integrates with both Zoho Books and QuickBooks on Essential+ and RelayPRO plans. Connect your existing account and your invoices sync automatically. You don't have to switch tools — we work with what you already have.
How does the automated review text message work?
Exactly 24 hours after a job is submitted, RelayAI automatically sends a friendly text message to your customer's phone number asking for a Google review. The message includes your business name and a direct link to your Google review page. It only sends once per job — no spam, no double sends. Customers can reply STOP to opt out at any time.
